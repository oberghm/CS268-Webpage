About Me

Hello!

My name is Molly Curtis and I’m a 21 year old portrait photographer from Minnesota. I’ve been doing photography for three years now and I’ve been blessed to work with so many amazing people. I started out by just taking photos of my friends my first year with my Sony a6300, now I’m shooting on a Sony a7rii. I’ve always loved to take photos but ever since I graduated high school, I got inspired to work more on the creative side. It took me a long time to figure out my style of photography. If you’re trying to get into photography, it definitely takes a lot of patience and time! Some things I like to do in my free time are going to the gym, cooking and spending time with friends. I also love to travel, especially if it is for photography. Other than photography, I’ve been a bartender and server for the past four years. I’m also in my third year of college for Marketing at University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire.
